# Design Brief

Direction: classic-modern bar, warm and welcoming, calm and slightly classy. The page should feel like entering Alco’s Bar and picking a bottle from the counter.

Priorities:

- Simple Linktree replacement, not a large creator website.
- Neon identity moment.
- Five bottle links: Twitch, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, X.
- Alcohol and mocktail friendly copy.
- Warm wood, amber light, red/gold neon, model-color accents.

Current temporary assets can be replaced later with Alco-provided art.
